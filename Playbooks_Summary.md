# Startup Playbooks - Executive Summary

## Quick Reference Guide
*15 Essential Playbooks Ordered by Strategic Impact*

## üéØ **High Impact - Foundation** (Must Do First)

### 1. Vision & Opportunity
**Why:** Foundation for everything else
- One-sentence vision statement
- Problem/mission brief with market timing
- TAM calculation and exit strategy
- **Doc:** Vision document (annual review)

### 2. Customer Discovery & Validation  
**Why:** Prevents building wrong product
- Customer interview scripts and feedback systems
- Problem-solution fit scorecard
- User personas and JTBD maps
- MVP test plans
- **Doc:** Customer discovery log (per iteration)

### 3. Business Model
**Why:** Ensures financial viability
- Revenue model and pricing strategy
- Unit economics calculator
- Market sizing and scalability projections
- **Doc:** Business model canvas (quarterly)

### 4. Product Strategy
**Why:** Converts insights to product direction
- Value proposition canvas
- 12-month product roadmap
- Feature prioritization framework
- Competitive differentiation matrix
- **Doc:** Product strategy doc (monthly review)

### 5. User Experience Design
**Why:** Great UX makes mediocre products successful
- User journey mapping and information architecture
- Wireframes, prototypes, and design system
- Usability testing framework
- Mobile/responsive strategy
- **Doc:** UX design system (per sprint)
